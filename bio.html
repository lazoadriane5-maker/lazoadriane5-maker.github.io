About Me

Adriane Lazo, known as MC_LĀZ, is a Filipino singer, songwriter, and producer whose music spans hip-hop, R&B, and pop. In addition to creating his own tracks, he is also involved in the production, mixing, and mastering of his music.

Notable Releases:
MC_LĀZ has a consistently updated discography, with several singles released in the last year. His notable works include: “MEDISINA” (2025), “KAWATAN” (feat. RealestLotti) (2025), “SHE’S INDEPENDENT” (feat. DENCHO) (2025), “TRIPLE TROUBLE” (ft. Dencho) (2025), “C,T,R (Chase The Rain)” (2024), and “Ang buwan sayo'y naninibugho” (2024).

Collaborations:
MC_LĀZ often collaborates with other artists, with frequent partnerships including: Dencho – a recurring collaborator on tracks such as “SHE’S INDEPENDENT” and “Hanggang kailan”; RealestLotti – featured on the single “KAWATAN”.

Online Presence:
He maintains an active online presence to promote his music and connect with fans. Social Media: updates are shared through Facebook and Instagram. Streaming Services: his work is available on Spotify, Apple Music, SoundCloud, Deezer, and Boomplay. A Linktree hub connects listeners to all his pages in one place.
